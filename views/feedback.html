Feedback & Issues

Encountered a bug? Did your search not return a result, or are you getting unexpected errors? Please contact us! We do our absolute best to ensure that SanctionsExplorer is bug-free and working correctly for everyone.

If you'd like to see a feature added, please contact us as well — we highly prioritize community input in our development.

Submit Feedback!

Please be as specific as you can. If a search did not work, please provide which filters were used and what you expected to see.

Feel free to include your name and email if you're okay with us possibly contacting you for additional details or clarifications. Including your email here does NOT subscribe you to our update list.
